# Petition 014 Outcome Contract

## Acceptance criteria

1. Every OpenDebt web site has its own accessibility statement.
2. Accessibility statements are discoverable to users.
3. Accessibility statements are maintained on change and at least annually.
4. Users can contact the responsible authority through accessible written and telephone channels.
5. The written contact path does not require inaccessible verification mechanisms.

## Definition of done

- Accessibility statements are part of UI rollout planning.
- Statement discoverability is testable in each UI.
- The contact path requirements are testable.
- Every acceptance criterion is covered by at least one Gherkin scenario.

## Failure conditions

- A UI is published without its own accessibility statement.
- Users cannot easily find the accessibility statement.
- The only written contact path requires MitID or inaccessible CAPTCHA.
- The statement is not updated after relevant change or annual review.
