# Civilretlige Fordringer (Civil-Law Claims)

General rules under gældsinddrivelsesloven (GIL) apply to civil-law claims. These claims have specific characteristics and limited collection options compared to public-law claims.

## Characteristics

- The creditor must be a **public authority**.
- The claim must arise from **public activity**.
- The claim must **not** originate from offentligretlig myndighedsudøvelse (public-law exercise of authority).

## Collection Steps

### 1. Afdragsordning (Instalment Arrangement)

- **Legal basis:** GIL §3 stk. 3
- No fundament (legal title) required.
- Gældsstyrelsen can establish an instalment plan with the debtor.

### 2. Lønindeholdelse (Wage Garnishment)

- **Legal basis:** GIL §10
- Generally **NOT available** for civil-law claims (see bilag 1 to GIL).

### 3. Modregning (Offsetting)

- **Requirements:** Claims must be gensidig (mutual), udjævnelig (fungible), forfalden (due), and retskraftig (legally enforceable).
- **GIL §8 indtrædelsesret** allows non-state creditors to offset against state payments owed to the debtor.

### 4. Udlæg (Seizure of Assets)

- **Legal basis:** GIL §11
- Generally **NOT available** for civil-law claims.
- **Exception:** Possible if an eksekutionsfundament (enforcement title) exists — e.g., dom (court judgment), betalingspåkrav (payment order), or skylderklæring (debt acknowledgement) — via retsplejeloven §478.

## Claims Without Fundament

If no enforcement title exists:

1. Attempt **modregning** (offsetting) and issue a **påkrav** (demand for payment).
2. If the debtor will not pay or acknowledge the debt, an **eksekutionsfundament** must be obtained.

## Betalingspåkrav (Payment Orders)

### Under 100,000 kr

Gældsstyrelsen can file a **betalingspåkrav** with the fogedret (enforcement court) on behalf of the creditor.

- **Legal basis:** Retsplejeloven §260 stk. 5 and §477b.
- A **påkravsskrivelse** (demand letter) must be sent to the debtor with a **10-day payment period**.
- If the debtor **objects**: the procedure stops, and objections are forwarded to the creditor for further action.

### Over 100,000 kr

- The creditor must file a **stævning** (lawsuit) themselves.
- Gældsstyrelsen returns the claim to the creditor.

## Limitation (Forældelse)

Civil-law claims have **limited options for forældelsesafbrydelse** (interruption of the limitation period) because pantefogedudlæg is not available.

Possible interruption methods:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Skyldserkendelse | Debtor acknowledges the debt |
| Betalingspåkrav | Filing a payment order with the court |
| Fogedret udlæg | Seizure via the enforcement court (requires fundament) |
| Modregning notification | Notifying the debtor of an offset |
