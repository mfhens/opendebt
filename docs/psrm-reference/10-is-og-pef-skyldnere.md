# I/S og PEF Skyldnere (Partnerships and Sole Proprietorships)

I/S (Interessentskab — general partnerships) and PEF (Personligt ejet firma — sole proprietorships) have **personal liability**, which affects how claims are submitted to PSRM.

## I/S (Interessentskab)

- Submit the **CVR number** for the partnership itself.
- **Also submit the CPR or CVR** for all partners who are personally liable at the time of claim creation.
- Collection can **only** proceed against the debtors explicitly submitted — if a partner is not included, Gældsstyrelsen cannot collect from them.
- Partner composition may vary over time; ensure the correct set of liable partners is submitted with each claim.

## PEF (Personligt Ejet Firma)

- If the business is **still active**: submit the **CVR number**.
- If the business has **ceased**: submit the owner's **CPR number** instead.
- PSRM **automatically adds the CPR** to PEF claims after submission, linking the personal liability to the business owner.
